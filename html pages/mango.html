MANGO

KESAR MANGO

Price:Rs 30

The Kesar mango has a distinctive fragrance to it that can liven up your house. It is cultivated in the Girnar mountains of Gujarat, Ahmedabad and named after the spice ‘saffron’.Season:June-July.Color: Skin is dull yellow with a green tinge and inside pulp is saffron-colored.

ALPHONSO

Price:Rs 60

Alphonso mango is not any other type of mango and is the king of all mangoes. It is a native to Maharashtra but also grown in some parts of Gujarat, Karnataka and Madhya Pradesh. It is the most expensive variety due to its distinct taste,color,and shape.Season: May-June.Color: Bright golden yellow with tinge of red Shape: Oval-shaped.Taste: Sweet & creamy texture.

TOTAPURI

Price:Rs 100

The Totapuri mango is the easiest type of mango to identify due to its parrot beak-like shape at the tip, hence symbolizing its name. It is from Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Telangana and Tamil Nadu i.e Southern part of India.Season: June to July.Color: Golden Yellow.Shape: Oblong with beak like pointed end .Taste: Not Sweet.Great for salads, pickles and mango juices.Large in size .Skin is very thick

HIMSAGAR

Price:Rs 120

Amongst all the varieties of mangoes, this variety has the shortest season span of them all. Himsagar originates in Northeast regions of India such as West Bengal.Season: May or June.Color: Green skin and bright yellow pulp.Shape: Ovate (egg like) shape.Taste: Extremely sweet.Thin skin & known for its aroma and Good keeping quality and Medium-sized and Fibreless and fleshy
